Description

A decadent doughnut recipe that you can easily make at home. Dipped in rich chocolate and cooled to perfection, you wouldn't need to go out to have your favourite chocolate doughnut anymore!

Ingredients

  • 500 gms flour
  • 100 gms sugar
  • 2 Egg yolk
  • 1/2 Egg
  • 100 gms butter
  • 25 gms yeast
  • 250 gms milk
  • Chocolate (to dip the doughnut), melted
  • Sprinkles of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix all together to make a dough and rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Roll the dough at the thickness of 2 inch then cut with round cutter.
  3. Allow the doughnut to prove till double the size.
  4. Deep fry the doughnut till it turns golden brown.
  5. Allow the doughnut to get cool and then dip in chocolate.
  6. Finally sprinkle your favourite sprinkles!
